The Vegenator

Red cabbage, like all the cabbage family, is a funny beast. Planted at the same time I guarantee they will grow at different rates. They will also attract pests that will need to be repelled with insecticidal soap. Beyond that I love them.

They prefer potatoes and onions. Especially potatoes. You will find the red cabbage closest to the potatoes will thrive the best. They are complimentary to garlic and chives but are antagonistic to strawberries so beware.
