The Vegenator
>

The Man, The Myth, The Legend

During the day the Vegenator is a mild-manner software developer and architect. He constructs software for finance professionals to make obscene amounts of money which is used to purchase sports cars, trophy spouses and Republican Congressmen.

By night he is the Vegenator... He can grow food within buckets, inbetween sidewalk cracks and on the front-lawn. No seedling is in danger whilst the Vegenator stalks the streets of Suburbia. He does after all own three water barrels.

But evil always exists in the form of aphids, insects and caterpillars. And worst of all, the Vegenator's evil nemesis, his Dr. Moriarty, embodiment of all that is unholy: the chipmunk.

In the brightest day, in the blackest night, no critter shall escape my sight, Let those who worship varmint's might, Beware my power, THE VEGENATOR'S SPITE!
